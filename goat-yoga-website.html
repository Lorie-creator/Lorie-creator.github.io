Goat Yoga in North Carolina

About Us

Goat yoga is a unique fitness experience that combines traditional yoga practice with the playful interaction of adorable goats. Originating as a creative farm activity, goat yoga has grown into a popular wellness trend that brings joy, laughter, and unexpected therapeutic benefits.

Benefits of Goat Yoga

  • Stress Reduction: Interacting with animals can lower cortisol levels
  • Improved Mental Health: Goat interactions promote happiness and reduce anxiety
  • Unique Exercise Experience: Adds fun and unpredictability to yoga practice

Safety and Sanitary Concerns

We prioritize participant and animal safety:

  • Goats are regularly health-checked and vaccinated
  • Yoga mats are sanitized before and after each session
  • Hand sanitization stations are available
  • Participants wear closed-toe shoes
